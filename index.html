The official travel site of the friendly Taniti islands BULA! If you ever visit Taniti, you’ll hear that a lot. It’s more than just a greeting, it’s the spirit of the country. With 333 islands, white sand beaches and year-round tropical warmth, it is hard not to feel happy in this Pacific Island nation. If you’re looking for a romantic getaway, an adventure-packed escape, or you just want to lie on the beach and order from a menu, you’ll find it in Taniti.
